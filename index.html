about me

My fascination with human consciousness guided my undergraduate studies in cognitive science before centering on sleep research during my five years at a sleep & chronobiology research lab.

Sleep is intimately related with nearly every facet of successful waking life functioning, and I am particularly interested in the intersection of information technologies & sleep health.

As a first year master's student at the UC Berkeley iSchool, my current curriculum is focused on user experience research & design, which I'm supplementing with coursework & self-study in topics related to product management.

After graduation, I aspire to work in health tech, ideally with a focus on sleep.
